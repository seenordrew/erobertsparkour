Discovery Bay Workshop

This beginner’s workshop is designed to be an accessible introduction to parkour and movement. We will jump, climb, and crawl using various skills and techniques. Discover how to move over, under, and through all kinds of obstacles as we explore various kinds of terrain. Parkour is a discipline that develops physical and internal capabilities that are useful to everyone in real life, and is fully adaptable to every body (and everybody). Beginner’s workshop is a fun and non-competitive environment where we work together to help each other explore, experience, and play in order to learn to overcome obstacles, both external and internal.
